The types of personal information we obtain about you depends on how you interact with our Site and use our Services. When we use the term "personal information", we are referring to information that identifies, relates to, describes or can be associated with you.

Information that you directly submit to us through our Services may include:

Google Analytics and Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to help us understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized information about:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our website (search engines, direct visits, etc.)
  • Your general location (country/city level, not precise location)

Your Privacy is Protected: We have configured Google Analytics with privacy-focused settings including IP anonymization and secure cookie flags. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

Cookie Consent: When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept or decline analytics cookies. Your choice is saved and respected across all pages.

Managing Cookies: You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using your browser's privacy settings. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
