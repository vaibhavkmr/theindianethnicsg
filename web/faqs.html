Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

To place an order, simply browse our collection on the website, click on any saree you like, and use the "Enquire via WhatsApp" button to connect with us directly. Our team will assist you with the ordering process, confirm pricing, and arrange payment and delivery through WhatsApp.

  • Singapore Orders: 2 to 5 business days
  • International Orders: 4 to 30 business days

You can reach us through:

  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210 (Preferred method)
  • Email: support@theindianethnic.sg
  • Contact Form: Use the Contact Us page on our website

  • Singapore: Free shipping (Standard delivery)
  • International Orders: Shipping charges will be confirmed via WhatsApp based on your delivery address and order weight.

We will send you order confirmation and tracking number via WhatsApp or email once your order is shipped. You can use the tracking link to monitor your delivery. Alternatively, you may contact us directly via WhatsApp at +91 98765 43210 for updates.

For international orders, custom duty shall be borne by the customers. Each country has their own customs regulations and we won't be able to confirm the final cost. Please check with your local customs office for estimated duties and taxes.

All sarees at TheIndianEthnic are ready stock. We will ship out immediately once we receive your order confirmation and payment. If there is any delay in shipping, it will be due to external factors from the shipping carrier.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, PayNow, and other secure online payment options. Our team will provide you with payment details and instructions via WhatsApp when you place your order.

If the package arrived tampered, DO NOT accept the package. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +91 98765 43210 or email at support@theindianethnic.sg. Please refer to our Refund and Shipping Policy for more information.

If you receive a defective product, please contact us immediately within 24 hours of delivery via WhatsApp at +91 98765 43210 or email at support@theindianethnic.sg. Please provide your Order ID and photos of the defect. For full details on our refund policy, please visit our Refund and Shipping Policy page.
